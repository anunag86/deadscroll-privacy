Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

DeadScroll ("we," "our," or "the App") is a digital wellness tool designed to introduce cognitive friction and prevent doomscrolling. We believe that your attention belongs to you, and so does your data. This Privacy Policy explains how the App operates and explicitly outlines our zero-data-collection architecture.

2. Accessibility Service API (Crucial Disclosure)

DeadScroll requires the use of the Android AccessibilityService API to function.

Why it is needed: The API is used strictly to detect when you launch a user-designated "target app" (e.g., social media) so that DeadScroll can draw its intervention overlay (the "Tollbooth") on top of the screen.

What it does NOT do: We do not use the AccessibilityService API to collect, store, or transmit any personal data, passwords, keystrokes, or screen content. The App only reads package names to determine if a targeted app has been opened.

3. Data Collection and Storage

DeadScroll operates entirely offline.

Zero Cloud Storage: The App does not connect to any external servers, databases, or analytics engines.

Local Data Only: Any data generated by the App (such as the list of apps you want to block, your chosen severity level, and your analytics regarding time saved) is stored locally on your device's internal storage using standard Android databases.

No Tracking: We do not use third-party trackers, crashlytics, or advertising IDs.

4. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect your data, we cannot share, sell, or distribute it to any third parties. Your usage history never leaves your phone.

5. User Consent and Control

You have full control over the App. You can revoke the Accessibility permission at any time via your device's System Settings, which will immediately pause the App's core functionality. You can also delete the App, which will permanently erase all local analytics and settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the technical architecture of DeadScroll, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository where this project is hosted.
